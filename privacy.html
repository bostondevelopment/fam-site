Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026 · Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Fam mobile application ("Fam", "we", "our") collects from you, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. Fam is operated by Boston Development. If you have questions, email bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com.

The short version. Fam exists to help families keep their people, photos, and events together privately. We don't sell your data, we don't show you ads, and we don't track you across other apps or websites. Everything you put into Fam is visible only to members of your family group, plus the small set of service providers below that we need to run the app.

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to people who create an account in the Fam mobile app on iOS or Android, and to family members whose information is added by someone else who has an account. It does not apply to other apps, websites, or services we do not operate.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us

  • Account information. Your email address and a password (which we store only as a salted hash — we never see your password in plain text). Optional first and last name.
  • Profile information. Anything you add to your own profile, including a profile photo, birthdate, location (city / state), notes, and relationships.
  • Family content. Information you add about other family members (name, birthdate, date of passing, gender, location, relationships, notes, obituary or memorial text), photos you upload, tags you apply to photos, events and occasions you create, RSVPs, comments, and notification preferences.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and network data. Push notification tokens (issued by Apple or Google via Expo), your IP address when you make sensitive requests like password reset, and your device's user-agent string.
  • Authentication tokens. Short-lived access tokens and longer-lived refresh tokens are issued when you sign in so you don't have to log in every time you open the app.
  • Diagnostic data. When the app crashes or hits an unexpected error, we collect crash and error reports through Sentry so we can fix the bug. These reports include a request identifier, stack traces, and the type of action you were taking; we do not include your password, your photos, or the content of your messages.

2.3 Information we derive

  • Face recognition for photo tagging. When you upload a photo, we send it to Amazon Rekognition so the app can detect faces and help you tag the right family member. Amazon stores a mathematical face vector (not the photo itself) in a private collection for your family. We use this only to suggest tags inside your family group. We do not use it for advertising, identity verification, or any purpose outside Fam. Face vectors are deleted when the underlying photo, person, or family is deleted.
  • Approximate photo dates and decades. The app may estimate when a photo was taken based on file metadata to help group old photos by year or decade. You can correct these estimates.

We do not use your information for advertising, do not sell or rent it to third parties, and do not track you across other apps or websites.

5. How we protect your information

  • All traffic between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS.
  • Passwords are stored only as a salted hash; we cannot recover them.
  • Photos are served via signed CloudFront URLs or cookies that expire so they cannot be casually re-shared.
  • Access to production systems is limited to a small number of authorized people and requires strong authentication.
  • We use error monitoring (Sentry) to detect failures quickly, and we scrub sensitive fields out of crash reports.

No system can be perfectly secure. If you become aware of a security issue, please write to bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com.

6. How long we keep your information

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account from inside the app (Settings → Delete Account), we permanently delete your profile, your photos, your family content that no other member depends on, and your derived face vectors within thirty (30) days. Encrypted database backups may retain a copy for a short rolling window after which they roll off.

We may keep a minimal record of the fact that an account was deleted (so we can avoid re-creating it) and any information we are legally required to retain.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Access and correction. You can view and edit your profile, your family content, and your notification preferences directly in the app.
  • Deletion. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This is permanent.
  • Push notifications. You can turn off any category of push notification in Settings → Notifications, and you can disable all notifications at the operating-system level.
  • Email. Transactional email (welcome, password reset) is part of operating the account. We do not send marketing email.
  • Requests. Email bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com if you need help, a copy of your data, or to delete data we hold about you.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights — for example, the right to access, correct, port, or delete your data, the right to object to certain processing, or the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority. We honor these rights regardless of where you live; please write to the email above.

8. Information about other people

Fam is a tool for documenting a family. When you add a family member, you are providing information about another person. You agree that you have a reasonable basis to do so (for example: you are documenting your own family for your family's private use), and you agree to remove information about a living family member if they ask you to. Information about deceased family members may be kept as part of the family record unless a close relative asks us to remove it.

9. Children's privacy

Fam is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly let children under 13 create an account. Adult family members may add information about minors — including children — to family profiles, so that the family can preserve memories and document relationships. If you are a parent or guardian and would like a child's information removed, write to bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com and we will remove it promptly.

10. International users

Fam is operated from the United States and our service providers are located in the United States. If you are using Fam from outside the United States, you are sending information into the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

11. Cookies and tracking

The Fam mobile app does not use cookies in the traditional web sense. Photo delivery uses short-lived signed cookies from Amazon CloudFront that exist only to authorize image requests for your family's content; they contain no personal information about you and are not used for analytics or advertising. This website does not set any tracking or advertising cookies.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Fam evolves. When we make a material change, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you by email or inside the app. Continued use of Fam after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
